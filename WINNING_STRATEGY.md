## 🏅 Winning Tips

### Before Presentation
1. **Practice**: Rehearse multiple times
2. **Know Your Numbers**: Have metrics ready
3. **Prepare Demo**: Test everything
4. **Dress Professional**: Make good impression
5. **Arrive Early**: Setup and test
6. **Bring Materials**: Business cards, brochures
7. **Know Your Team**: Be ready to introduce
8. **Have Backup**: USB with presentation

### During Presentation
1. **Start Strong**: Grab attention immediately
2. **Tell Story**: Make it emotional
3. **Show Demo**: Live is better than slides
4. **Make Eye Contact**: Engage audience
5. **Speak Clearly**: Pace and volume
6. **Use Gestures**: Be animated
7. **Answer Confidently**: Know your stuff
8. **End Strong**: Clear call to action

### After Presentation
1. **Network**: Talk to judges
2. **Exchange Contact**: Get their info
3. **Follow Up**: Send thank you
4. **Gather Feedback**: Learn from judges
5. **Celebrate**: Acknowledge team effort
6. **Plan Next**: Prepare for next round
